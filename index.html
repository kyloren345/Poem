My Poem

Roses are red, violets are blue,
Sugar is sweet, and so are you.
In this world, where troubles abound,
Your smile and laughter, a joyful sound.

Through ups and downs, we will stand strong,
Forever together, where we belong.
Hand in hand, we'll brave each day,
Love's guiding light, showing the way.

As seasons change and years go by,
Our love will grow, reaching the sky.
With each passing moment, our bond will thrive,
Forever entwined, hearts alive.
